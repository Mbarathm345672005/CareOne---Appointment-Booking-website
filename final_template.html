FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Care One different from other clinics? expand_more
We combine board-certified medical expertise with the latest FDA-approved aesthetic technologies. Our focus is on holistic patient outcomes rather than just temporary fixes.
Are the hair restoration treatments permanent? expand_more
Yes, many of our surgical and clinical hair restoration methods are designed for long-term permanence. During your consultation, we'll discuss the expected results based on your specific condition.
How long is the recovery time for laser treatments? expand_more
Most of our modern laser therapies have minimal downtime. Some patients experience slight redness for 24-48 hours, but most return to their daily routine immediately after the session.
